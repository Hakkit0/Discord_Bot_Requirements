Aphr Bot - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Aphr Bot does not store or permanently collect personal data. The Bot may temporarily access user IDs, usernames, message content, server IDs, and role information only for the purpose of executing commands.

2. How Information Is Used

Information processed by the Bot is used solely for providing bot functionality, such as responding to commands, sending automated messages, and enabling server features.

3. Data Storage

By default, the Bot does not store any user data. If server administrators enable logging or configuration storage, this data remains within the server and is not shared externally.

4. Third-Party Access

No data is shared with third parties. All data processed by the Bot remains within Discord’s platform and API.

5. Children’s Privacy

Aphr Bot adheres to Discord's age requirements. Users under the permitted age may not use the Bot.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the Bot indicates acceptance of any updated policy.

7. Contact

If you have questions or concerns, please contact the bot owner or server administrators.
